# Alert Color Coding Guide

## Quick Reference: Know If It's Bull or Bear Instantly! 🎯

### 🟢 GREEN = BULLISH/LONG (Good for buying)

| Alert | What It Means | Action |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| **BUY WALL FORMING** 🧱 | Large buy orders stacking up | Support building - bullish |
| **BULLISH REJECTION** 🎯 | Price bouncing off support | Buyers defending level |
| **BULLISH DIVERGENCE** 📊 | Price falling but buyers accumulating | Hidden accumulation - reversal likely |
| **WHALE BUYING** 🐋 | Large whale buying detected | Smart money entering |
| **INSTITUTIONAL BUY** 🏛 | Institutional-sized buy order | Professional traders buying |
| **STRONG BUY PRESSURE** 💪 | Aggressive buying detected | Momentum to upside |
| **ABSORPTION** 🧽 | Buyers absorbing all sell orders | Strong demand at this level |

**Visual Cues:**
- Green left border on alert chip
- Green price text
- Green note text
- 🟢 emoji in Telegram
- Voice says "Bullish" before alert type

---

### 🔴 RED = BEARISH/SHORT (Good for selling)

| Alert | What It Means | Action |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| **SELL WALL FORMING** 🧱 | Large sell orders stacking up | Resistance building - bearish |
| **BEARISH REJECTION** 🎯 | Price rejecting from resistance | Sellers defending level |
| **BEARISH DIVERGENCE** 📊 | Price rising but sellers distributing | Hidden distribution - reversal likely |
| **WHALE SELLING** 🐋 | Large whale selling detected | Smart money exiting |
| **INSTITUTIONAL SELL** 🏛 | Institutional-sized sell order | Professional traders selling |
| **STRONG SELL PRESSURE** 💥 | Aggressive selling detected | Momentum to downside |
| **ABSORPTION** 🧽 | Sellers absorbing all buy orders | Strong supply at this level |

**Visual Cues:**
- Red left border on alert chip
- Red price text
- Red note text
- 🔴 emoji in Telegram
- Voice says "Bearish" before alert type

---

### ⚪ WHITE/NEUTRAL = WARNING

| Alert | What It Means | Action |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| **SPOOFING SUSPECTED** ⚠️ | Orders appearing/disappearing quickly | Market manipulation warning |

**Visual Cues:**
- No colored border
- White/dim text
- ⚪ emoji in Telegram
- Voice doesn't say "Bullish" or "Bearish"

## Pro Tips

1. **Green alerts during uptrend** = Confirmation of bullish momentum
2. **Red alerts during downtrend** = Confirmation of bearish momentum
3. **Green alerts during downtrend** = Potential reversal signal (divergence/absorption)
4. **Red alerts during uptrend** = Potential reversal signal (divergence/rejection)
5. **Multiple green alerts** = Strong bullish conviction
6. **Multiple red alerts** = Strong bearish conviction
7. **Mixed colors** = Market indecision/choppy conditions
