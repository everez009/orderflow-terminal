# Orderflow v4 - Quick Start Guide (2 Minutes)

## 🚀 Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the File
Double-click `orderflow_1304262038-v4.html` or drag it into your browser.

**That's it!** No installation, no setup, no accounts needed.

---

### Step 2: Set Your Risk (Top Right Panel)

You'll see a panel called **"TRADE MANAGER"** on the right side.

1. **Account Balance:** Type how much money you have (e.g., `10000`)
2. **Risk Per Trade:** Leave at `1%` (safe) or change to `2%` (moderate)

The system automatically calculates:
- How much to buy/sell
- Where to put stop loss
- Where to take profit
- Your risk/reward ratio

---

### Step 3: Wait for Signals

The system scans the market automatically. When it finds a good setup:

**You'll see:**
- 🔊 Sound alert (if enabled)
- 🟢 BUY or 🔴 SELL signal bar
- ⭐ Star rating (more stars = better)
- 📝 Entry in Trade Journal (automatic)

**What to do:**
1. Check the star rating (★★★★★ is best)
2. Look at Risk Calculator for position size
3. Enter the trade with suggested size
4. Set stop loss and take profit as shown
5. Monitor your trade in the Journal

---

## 📊 Understanding the 3 Panels

### Left Panel: Volume Profile
Shows where most trading happened (important price levels)

### Middle Panel: Key Levels  
Shows support and resistance zones (where price might bounce)

### Right Panel: Trade Manager (NEW!)
- 💰 **Risk Calculator** - Tells you how much to trade
- 📈 **Performance Stats** - Shows if you're winning
- 📒 **Trade Journal** - Records all your signals

---

## 💡 Quick Tips

### ✅ DO:
- Only trade 4★ and 5★ signals (higher win rate)
- Use the position size from Risk Calculator
- Keep risk at 1-2% per trade
- Review your stats weekly
- Let winners run to target

### ❌ DON'T:
- Trade B-quality setups (≤3 stars)
- Risk more than 2% on one trade
- Move your stop loss further away
- Chase losses after a bad trade
- Ignore the journal (learn from it!)

## 🎯 Example Trade

**Signal Appears:**
```
🟢 BUY SIGNAL ★★★★★
Price: $71,500
```

**Risk Calculator Shows:**
```
Position Size: 0.3333 BTC
Stop Loss: $71,200
Take Profit: $72,200
Risk/Reward: 1:2.3 🟢
```

**You Do:**
1. Buy 0.3333 BTC at $71,500
2. Set stop loss at $71,200
3. Set take profit at $72,200
4. Wait for result

**Result:**
- If hits $72,200 → You make $230 ✅
- If hits $71,200 → You lose $100 ❌
- Either way, you followed the plan!
